This plugin add BookYourself online reservation system to your page.

== Description ==

BookYourself online reservations is easy way to add an option for your customers to book your services directly from your wordpress page

Just add shortcode with settings of width and your provider ID on BookYourself.com.
Eg.:
[reservations width: 650 provider: 111]

The basic functionalities of BookYourself online reservation system are online reservations, group term (group exercises, therapies, courses,...), notifications, link with google calendar.

== Installation ==

1. Log in to your website administrator panel.
1. Go to Plugins page, and add new plugin.
1. Upload or search BookYourself online reservations.
1. Click Install Now button.
1. Then click Activate Plugin button.
